CESC Mysuru Smart Meter Recharge — How to Recharge Online

Everything you need to recharge a Chamundeshwari Electricity Supply Corporation Ltd. prepaid smart meter online — across Mysuru, Mandya, Hassan, Chamarajanagar, plus a units-per-recharge estimate computed from the real CESC Mysuru tariff rates.

How to recharge a CESC Mysuru smart meter

  1. 1
    Open the recharge channelRecharge on the official CESC Mysuru portal at cescmysore.karnataka.gov.in, or through BBPS-enabled UPI apps (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm etc.) by selecting "CESC Mysuru".
  2. 2
    Enter your consumer / meter numberYou'll find it on your smart meter card, an old bill, or the meter's display.
  3. 3
    Pick an amountUse the table below to gauge roughly how many units your money buys.
  4. 4
    PayUPI, debit/credit card or net-banking — keep the payment receipt.
  5. 5
    Watch the balance updateThe balance usually updates within minutes; occasionally it can take a few hours. Check the meter display or the app.

A rough estimate computed from CESC Mysuru's real domestic slab rates (₹5.8–₹5.8/unit). Remember — your prepaid balance doesn't only pay energy charges: fixed charges, FPPA and electricity duty are deducted daily too, so actual units will be somewhat lower.

What happens when the balance runs low or out?

You get low-balance alerts (SMS/app notification) from the meter. If the balance goes negative, supply can be disconnected automatically — though most DISCOMs do not disconnect at night, on Sundays or on holidays (rules vary by DISCOM). Once you recharge, supply is normally restored automatically with no separate reconnection fee. Full details in our guide: prepaid smart meter disconnection rules →

Frequently asked questions

How do I recharge my CESC Mysuru smart meter?
Recharge on the official CESC Mysuru portal at cescmysore.karnataka.gov.in, or through BBPS-enabled UPI apps (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm etc.) by selecting "CESC Mysuru". Pay by UPI, card or net-banking; the balance usually updates within minutes.
How many units does a ₹500 recharge give on CESC Mysuru?
CESC Mysuru's domestic rate spans ₹5.8–₹5.8 per unit, so ₹500 buys roughly 86–86 units — a little less after fixed charges, FPPA and duty are deducted.
What is the minimum recharge amount?
The minimum varies by DISCOM — the exact limit is shown on the official portal/app at recharge time. Rather than many small top-ups, one recharge sized to your typical monthly consumption is usually more convenient.
I recharged but power hasn't come back — what now?
After a successful payment, supply is normally restored automatically; give it a few minutes. If not, check the balance on the meter display, keep your receipt, and raise a complaint on 1912 or your DISCOM helpline.
