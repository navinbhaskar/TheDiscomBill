Where your units go

Estimates assume a 30-day month. Refrigerator and AC hours mean effective compressor run-time, not clock hours (a fridge cycles on/off). Actual consumption varies with appliance efficiency (star rating), ambient temperature and usage. Treat this as a planning guide, then use the bill calculator for an exact, tariff-accurate amount.
