Frequently asked questions

How is the electricity bill calculated in Ladakh?
Ladakh bills combine slab-wise energy charges, a fixed/demand charge per kW (or kVA), a fuel & power purchase adjustment (FPPA) and electricity duty. Use our free calculator to get an itemised provisional bill for your DISCOM.
Which electricity distribution company serves my area in Ladakh?
Ladakh is served by 1 DISCOM: LPDCL / Ladakh Power Dept. (Leh, Kargil districts). Open your DISCOM above for its full tariff and an indicative bill.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate in Ladakh?
The lowest domestic energy charge across Ladakh DISCOMs starts at about ₹0.5 per unit (lowest slab), before fixed charges, FPPA and duty. Exact rates depend on your DISCOM and consumption slab.
