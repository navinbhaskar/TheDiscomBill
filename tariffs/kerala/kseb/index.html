Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate my KSEB electricity bill?
Open the KSEB bill calculator, enter your units consumed and sanctioned load, and it applies the 2024-25 KSEB slab rates, fixed charge and other charges to produce an itemised provisional bill.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate on KSEB?
KSEB's domestic energy charge starts at ₹2.9 per unit and rises to ₹9.5 per unit in the highest slab (LT-1A (Domestic – load < 500W)), plus a monthly fixed charge (latest available estimate). The full slab table is above.
How do I pay my KSEB electricity bill online?
Pay on the official KSEB portal at www.kseb.in. Use this page first to check what your bill should be, then pay on the authentic source.

Figures are provisional estimates built on publicly available Kerala tariff orders. Always verify against your official KSEB bill — rates vary by sub-category, slab and city.
