Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate my UHBVN electricity bill?
Open the UHBVN bill calculator, enter your units consumed and sanctioned load, and it applies the 2024-25 UHBVN slab rates, fixed charge and other charges to produce an itemised provisional bill.
Which areas and cities does UHBVN serve?
UHBVN (Uttar Haryana Bijli Vitran Nigam Ltd.) supplies electricity to North Haryana — Ambala, Kurukshetra, Karnal, Sonipat, Panipat, Yamunanagar, Hisar, Rohtak.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate on UHBVN?
UHBVN's domestic energy charge starts at ₹2 per unit and rises to ₹6 per unit in the highest slab (DS (Domestic Supply)), plus a monthly fixed charge (latest available estimate). The full slab table is above.
How do I pay my UHBVN electricity bill online?
Pay on the official UHBVN portal at www.uhbvn.org.in. Use this page first to check what your bill should be, then pay on the authentic source.
