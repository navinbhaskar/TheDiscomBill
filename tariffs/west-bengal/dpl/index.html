Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate my DPL electricity bill?
Open the DPL bill calculator, enter your units consumed and sanctioned load, and it applies the 2024-25 DPL slab rates, fixed charge and other charges to produce an itemised provisional bill.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate on DPL?
DPL's domestic energy charge starts at ₹4.5 per unit and rises to ₹8.5 per unit in the highest slab (LT Domestic), plus a monthly fixed charge (latest available estimate). The full slab table is above.
How do I pay my DPL electricity bill online?
Pay on the official DPL portal at www.dpl.co.in. Use this page first to check what your bill should be, then pay on the authentic source.
