Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate my WBSEDCL electricity bill?
Open the WBSEDCL bill calculator, enter your units consumed and sanctioned load, and it applies the 2024-25 WBSEDCL slab rates, fixed charge and other charges to produce an itemised provisional bill.
Which areas and cities does WBSEDCL serve?
WBSEDCL (West Bengal State Electricity Distribution Company Ltd.) supplies electricity to All of West Bengal except CESC area — Kolkata city and DPL area.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate on WBSEDCL?
WBSEDCL's domestic energy charge starts at ₹4.61 per unit and rises to ₹8.84 per unit in the highest slab (LT-A1 (Domestic / Residential)), plus a monthly fixed charge (latest available estimate). The full slab table is above.
How do I pay my WBSEDCL electricity bill online?
Pay on the official WBSEDCL portal at www.wbsedcl.in. Use this page first to check what your bill should be, then pay on the authentic source.
