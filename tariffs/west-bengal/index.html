Frequently asked questions

How is the electricity bill calculated in West Bengal?
West Bengal bills combine slab-wise energy charges, a fixed/demand charge per kW (or kVA), a fuel & power purchase adjustment (FPPA) and electricity duty. Use our free calculator to get an itemised provisional bill for your DISCOM.
Which electricity distribution company serves my area in West Bengal?
West Bengal is served by 3 DISCOMs: WBSEDCL (All of West Bengal except CESC area — Kolkata city and DPL area); CESC (Kolkata) (Kolkata city, Howrah, Hooghly — parts); DPL (Durgapur industrial and urban area). Open your DISCOM above for its full tariff and an indicative bill.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate in West Bengal?
The lowest domestic energy charge across West Bengal DISCOMs starts at about ₹4.5 per unit (lowest slab), before fixed charges, FPPA and duty. Exact rates depend on your DISCOM and consumption slab.
What is the current electricity tariff year for West Bengal?
The rates shown are for 2024-25 (latest available).
