Frequently asked questions

How is the electricity bill calculated in Madhya Pradesh?
Madhya Pradesh bills combine slab-wise energy charges, a fixed/demand charge per kW (or kVA), a fuel & power purchase adjustment (FPPA) and electricity duty. Use our free calculator to get an itemised provisional bill for your DISCOM.
Which electricity distribution company serves my area in Madhya Pradesh?
Madhya Pradesh is served by 3 DISCOMs: MPPKVVCL (West-Central MP — Indore, Ujjain, Ratlam); MPMKVVCL (Central MP — Bhopal, Sagar, Rewa); MPEZ (East MP — Jabalpur, Chhindwara, Seoni). Open your DISCOM above for its full tariff and an indicative bill.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate in Madhya Pradesh?
The lowest domestic energy charge across Madhya Pradesh DISCOMs starts at about ₹2.05 per unit (lowest slab), before fixed charges, FPPA and duty. Exact rates depend on your DISCOM and consumption slab.
