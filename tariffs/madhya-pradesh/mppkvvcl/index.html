Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate my MPPKVVCL electricity bill?
Open the MPPKVVCL bill calculator, enter your units consumed and sanctioned load, and it applies the 2024-25 MPPKVVCL slab rates, fixed charge and other charges to produce an itemised provisional bill.
Which areas and cities does MPPKVVCL serve?
MPPKVVCL (Madhya Pradesh Paschim Kshetra Vidyut Vitaran Company Ltd.) supplies electricity to West-Central MP — Indore, Ujjain, Ratlam, Mandsaur, Dewas, Khandwa, Khargone.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate on MPPKVVCL?
MPPKVVCL's domestic energy charge starts at ₹2.05 per unit and rises to ₹6.55 per unit in the highest slab (LT-1 (Domestic / Residential)), plus a monthly fixed charge (latest available estimate). The full slab table is above.
How do I pay my MPPKVVCL electricity bill online?
Pay on the official MPPKVVCL portal at www.mppkvvcl.com. Use this page first to check what your bill should be, then pay on the authentic source.
