Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate my TSNPDCL electricity bill?
Open the TSNPDCL bill calculator, enter your units consumed and sanctioned load, and it applies the 2024-25 TSNPDCL slab rates, fixed charge and other charges to produce an itemised provisional bill.
Which areas and cities does TSNPDCL serve?
TSNPDCL (Telangana State Northern Power Distribution Company Ltd.) supplies electricity to Northern Telangana — Warangal, Karimnagar, Nizamabad, Adilabad, Khammam, Peddapalli, Rajanna Sircilla.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate on TSNPDCL?
TSNPDCL's domestic energy charge starts at ₹1.45 per unit and rises to ₹8.5 per unit in the highest slab (LT-I (Domestic / Residential)), plus a monthly fixed charge (latest available estimate). The full slab table is above.
How do I pay my TSNPDCL electricity bill online?
Pay on the official TSNPDCL portal at www.tsnpdcl.in. Use this page first to check what your bill should be, then pay on the authentic source.
