Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate my PVVNL electricity bill?
Open the PVVNL bill calculator, enter your units consumed and sanctioned load, and it applies the 2026-27 PVVNL slab rates, fixed charge, FPPA and electricity duty to produce an itemised provisional bill.
Which areas and cities does PVVNL serve?
PVVNL (Paschimanchal Vidyut Vitaran Nigam Ltd.) supplies electricity to West UP — Meerut, Ghaziabad, Noida, Hapur, Bulandshahr, Muzaffarnagar, Saharanpur, Shamli, Baghpat, Amroha, Moradabad, Rampur, Bijnor, Pilibhit, Shahjahanpur.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate on PVVNL?
PVVNL's domestic energy charge starts at ₹2.75 per unit and rises to ₹6.5 per unit in the highest slab (LMV-1 Residential / Domestic), plus a monthly fixed charge. The full slab table is above.
How do I pay my PVVNL electricity bill online?
Pay on the official PVVNL portal at www.pvvnl.org. Use this page first to check what your bill should be, then pay on the authentic source.
What is the late payment surcharge (LPSC) for PVVNL?
PVVNL levies a Late Payment Surcharge of 1.5% per month on overdue amounts. Our calculator can add LPSC and arrears to estimate your total payable.
