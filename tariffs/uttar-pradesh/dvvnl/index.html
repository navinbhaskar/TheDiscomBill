Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate my DVVNL electricity bill?
Open the DVVNL bill calculator, enter your units consumed and sanctioned load, and it applies the 2025-26 DVVNL slab rates, fixed charge, FPPA and electricity duty to produce an itemised provisional bill.
Which areas and cities does DVVNL serve?
DVVNL (Dakshinanchal Vidyut Vitaran Nigam Ltd.) supplies electricity to South UP — Agra, Mathura, Aligarh, Firozabad, Hathras, Etah, Mainpuri, Etawah, Farrukhabad, Kannauj, Jhansi, Lalitpur, Jalaun, Hamirpur, Mahoba, Banda, Chitrakoot.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate on DVVNL?
DVVNL's domestic energy charge starts at ₹3 per unit and rises to ₹6.5 per unit in the highest slab (LMV-1 Residential / Domestic), plus a monthly fixed charge. The full slab table is above.
How do I pay my DVVNL electricity bill online?
Pay on the official DVVNL portal at www.dvvnl.org. Use this page first to check what your bill should be, then pay on the authentic source.
What is the late payment surcharge (LPSC) for DVVNL?
DVVNL levies a Late Payment Surcharge of 1.5% per month on overdue amounts. Our calculator can add LPSC and arrears to estimate your total payable.
