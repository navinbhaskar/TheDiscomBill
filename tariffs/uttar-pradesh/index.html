Frequently asked questions

How is the electricity bill calculated in Uttar Pradesh?
Uttar Pradesh bills combine slab-wise energy charges, a fixed/demand charge per kW (or kVA), a fuel & power purchase adjustment (FPPA) and electricity duty. Use our free calculator to get an itemised provisional bill for your DISCOM.
Which electricity distribution company serves my area in Uttar Pradesh?
Uttar Pradesh is served by 5 DISCOMs: DVVNL (South UP — Agra, Mathura, Aligarh); MVVNL (Central UP — Lucknow, Sitapur, Hardoi); PVVNL (West UP — Meerut, Ghaziabad, Noida); PuVVNL (East UP — Varanasi, Prayagraj, Gorakhpur); KESCO (Kanpur city and district). Open your DISCOM above for its full tariff and an indicative bill.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate in Uttar Pradesh?
The lowest domestic energy charge across Uttar Pradesh DISCOMs starts at about ₹3 per unit (lowest slab), before fixed charges, FPPA and duty. Exact rates depend on your DISCOM and consumption slab.
