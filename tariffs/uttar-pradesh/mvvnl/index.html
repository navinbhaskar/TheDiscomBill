Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate my MVVNL electricity bill?
Open the MVVNL bill calculator, enter your units consumed and sanctioned load, and it applies the 2025-26 MVVNL slab rates, fixed charge, FPPA and electricity duty to produce an itemised provisional bill.
Which areas and cities does MVVNL serve?
MVVNL (Madhyanchal Vidyut Vitaran Nigam Ltd.) supplies electricity to Central UP — Lucknow, Sitapur, Hardoi, Unnao, Rae Bareli, Lakhimpur Kheri, Barabanki, Gonda, Balrampur, Shravasti, Bahraich.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate on MVVNL?
MVVNL's domestic energy charge starts at ₹3 per unit and rises to ₹6.5 per unit in the highest slab (LMV-1 Residential / Domestic), plus a monthly fixed charge. The full slab table is above.
How do I pay my MVVNL electricity bill online?
Pay on the official MVVNL portal at www.mvvnl.in. Use this page first to check what your bill should be, then pay on the authentic source.
What is the late payment surcharge (LPSC) for MVVNL?
MVVNL levies a Late Payment Surcharge of 1.5% per month on overdue amounts. Our calculator can add LPSC and arrears to estimate your total payable.
