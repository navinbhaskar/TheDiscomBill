Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate my MSEDCL electricity bill?
Open the MSEDCL bill calculator, enter your units consumed and sanctioned load, and it applies the 2024-25 MSEDCL slab rates, fixed charge and other charges to produce an itemised provisional bill.
Which areas and cities does MSEDCL serve?
MSEDCL (Maharashtra State Electricity Distribution Co. Ltd.) supplies electricity to Maharashtra — except Mumbai city area served by Adani/BEST/Tata Power.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate on MSEDCL?
MSEDCL's domestic energy charge starts at ₹3.46 per unit and rises to ₹11.13 per unit in the highest slab (LT-1 (Residential)), plus a monthly fixed charge (latest available estimate). The full slab table is above.
How do I pay my MSEDCL electricity bill online?
Pay on the official MSEDCL portal at www.mahadiscom.in. Use this page first to check what your bill should be, then pay on the authentic source.
