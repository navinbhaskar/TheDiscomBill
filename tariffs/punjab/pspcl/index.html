Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate my PSPCL electricity bill?
Open the PSPCL bill calculator, enter your units consumed and sanctioned load, and it applies the 2024-25 PSPCL slab rates, fixed charge and other charges to produce an itemised provisional bill.
Which areas and cities does PSPCL serve?
PSPCL (Punjab State Power Corporation Ltd.) supplies electricity to Entire Punjab — excl. Chandigarh.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate on PSPCL?
PSPCL's domestic energy charge starts at ₹3.49 per unit and rises to ₹6.89 per unit in the highest slab (DS (Domestic Supply)), plus a monthly fixed charge (latest available estimate). The full slab table is above.
How do I pay my PSPCL electricity bill online?
Pay on the official PSPCL portal at www.pspcl.in. Use this page first to check what your bill should be, then pay on the authentic source.
