Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate my UGVCL electricity bill?
Open the UGVCL bill calculator, enter your units consumed and sanctioned load, and it applies the 2024-25 UGVCL slab rates, fixed charge and other charges to produce an itemised provisional bill.
Which areas and cities does UGVCL serve?
UGVCL (Uttar Gujarat Vij Company Ltd.) supplies electricity to North Gujarat — Mehsana, Patan, Banaskantha, Sabarkantha, Gandhinagar, Aravalli.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate on UGVCL?
UGVCL's domestic energy charge starts at ₹1 per unit and rises to ₹5 per unit in the highest slab (LT-1 (Domestic / Residential)), plus a monthly fixed charge (latest available estimate). The full slab table is above.
How do I pay my UGVCL electricity bill online?
Pay on the official UGVCL portal at www.ugvcl.com. Use this page first to check what your bill should be, then pay on the authentic source.
