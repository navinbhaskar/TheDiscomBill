Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate my DGVCL electricity bill?
Open the DGVCL bill calculator, enter your units consumed and sanctioned load, and it applies the 2024-25 DGVCL slab rates, fixed charge and other charges to produce an itemised provisional bill.
Which areas and cities does DGVCL serve?
DGVCL (Dakshin Gujarat Vij Company Ltd.) supplies electricity to South Gujarat — Surat city, Navsari, Valsad, Tapi, Dang.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate on DGVCL?
DGVCL's domestic energy charge starts at ₹1 per unit and rises to ₹5 per unit in the highest slab (LT-1 (Domestic / Residential)), plus a monthly fixed charge (latest available estimate). The full slab table is above.
How do I pay my DGVCL electricity bill online?
Pay on the official DGVCL portal at www.dgvcl.com. Use this page first to check what your bill should be, then pay on the authentic source.
