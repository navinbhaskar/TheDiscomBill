Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate my TPCODL electricity bill?
Open the TPCODL bill calculator, enter your units consumed and sanctioned load, and it applies the 2024-25 TPCODL slab rates, fixed charge and other charges to produce an itemised provisional bill.
Which areas and cities does TPCODL serve?
TPCODL (TP Central Odisha Distribution Ltd. (Tata Power)) supplies electricity to Central Odisha — Bhubaneswar, Cuttack, Puri, Khordha, Jagatsinghpur, Kendrapara, Nayagarh, Angul, Dhenkanal.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate on TPCODL?
TPCODL's domestic energy charge starts at ₹2.1 per unit and rises to ₹6.2 per unit in the highest slab (LT-I (Domestic)), plus a monthly fixed charge (latest available estimate). The full slab table is above.
How do I pay my TPCODL electricity bill online?
Pay on the official TPCODL portal at www.tpcodl.in. Use this page first to check what your bill should be, then pay on the authentic source.
