Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate my TPWODL electricity bill?
Open the TPWODL bill calculator, enter your units consumed and sanctioned load, and it applies the 2026-27 TPWODL slab rates, fixed charge and other charges to produce an itemised provisional bill.
Which areas and cities does TPWODL serve?
TPWODL (TP Western Odisha Distribution Ltd. (Tata Power)) supplies electricity to West Odisha — Sambalpur, Bargarh, Boudh, Deogarh, Jharsuguda, Nuapada, Bolangir, Subarnapur.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate on TPWODL?
TPWODL's domestic energy charge starts at ₹0 per unit and rises to ₹6.1 per unit in the highest slab (LT Domestic), plus a monthly fixed charge (latest available estimate). The full slab table is above.
How do I pay my TPWODL electricity bill online?
Pay on the official TPWODL portal at www.tpwesternodisha.com. Use this page first to check what your bill should be, then pay on the authentic source.
