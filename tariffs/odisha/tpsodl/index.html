Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate my TPSODL electricity bill?
Open the TPSODL bill calculator, enter your units consumed and sanctioned load, and it applies the 2024-25 TPSODL slab rates, fixed charge and other charges to produce an itemised provisional bill.
Which areas and cities does TPSODL serve?
TPSODL (TP Southern Odisha Distribution Ltd. (Tata Power)) supplies electricity to South Odisha — Ganjam, Gajapati, Kandhamal, Kalahandi, Koraput, Nabarangpur, Rayagada, Malkangiri.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate on TPSODL?
TPSODL's domestic energy charge starts at ₹2.1 per unit and rises to ₹6.2 per unit in the highest slab (LT-I (Domestic)), plus a monthly fixed charge (latest available estimate). The full slab table is above.
How do I pay my TPSODL electricity bill online?
Pay on the official TPSODL portal at www.tpsodl.in. Use this page first to check what your bill should be, then pay on the authentic source.
