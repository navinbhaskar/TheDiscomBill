Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate my TANGEDCO electricity bill?
Open the TANGEDCO bill calculator, enter your units consumed and sanctioned load, and it applies the 2024-25 TANGEDCO slab rates, fixed charge and other charges to produce an itemised provisional bill.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate on TANGEDCO?
TANGEDCO's domestic energy charge starts at ₹0 per unit and rises to ₹7 per unit in the highest slab (LT-1 (Domestic – Bimonthly Billing)), plus a monthly fixed charge (latest available estimate). The full slab table is above.
How do I pay my TANGEDCO electricity bill online?
Pay on the official TANGEDCO portal at www.tangedco.gov.in. Use this page first to check what your bill should be, then pay on the authentic source.
