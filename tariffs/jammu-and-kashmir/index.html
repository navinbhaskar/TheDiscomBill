Frequently asked questions

How is the electricity bill calculated in Jammu & Kashmir?
Jammu & Kashmir bills combine slab-wise energy charges, a fixed/demand charge per kW (or kVA), a fuel & power purchase adjustment (FPPA) and electricity duty. Use our free calculator to get an itemised provisional bill for your DISCOM.
Which electricity distribution company serves my area in Jammu & Kashmir?
Jammu & Kashmir is served by 2 DISCOMs: JKPDD (Jammu Region) (Jammu, Samba, Kathua, Udhampur, Reasi, Ramban, Doda, Kishtwar, Rajouri, Poonch); JKPDD (Kashmir Region) (Srinagar, Baramulla, Anantnag, Kupwara, Pulwama, Kulgam, Ganderbal, Shopian, Bandipora). Open your DISCOM above for its full tariff and an indicative bill.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate in Jammu & Kashmir?
The lowest domestic energy charge across Jammu & Kashmir DISCOMs starts at about ₹1.5 per unit (lowest slab), before fixed charges, FPPA and duty. Exact rates depend on your DISCOM and consumption slab.
