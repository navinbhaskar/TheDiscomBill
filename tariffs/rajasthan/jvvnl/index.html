Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate my JVVNL electricity bill?
Open the JVVNL bill calculator, enter your units consumed and sanctioned load, and it applies the 2024-25 JVVNL slab rates, fixed charge and other charges to produce an itemised provisional bill.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate on JVVNL?
JVVNL's domestic energy charge starts at ₹3 per unit and rises to ₹6.5 per unit in the highest slab (LT-1 (Domestic / Residential)), plus a monthly fixed charge (latest available estimate). The full slab table is above.
How do I pay my JVVNL electricity bill online?
Pay on the official JVVNL portal at www.jaipurdiscom.com. Use this page first to check what your bill should be, then pay on the authentic source.
