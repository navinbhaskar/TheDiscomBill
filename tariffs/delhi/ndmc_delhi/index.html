Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate my NDMC electricity bill?
Open the NDMC bill calculator, enter your units consumed and sanctioned load, and it applies the 2024-25 NDMC slab rates, fixed charge and other charges to produce an itemised provisional bill.
Which areas and cities does NDMC serve?
NDMC (New Delhi Municipal Council) supplies electricity to Lutyens' Delhi — Central Government area.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate on NDMC?
NDMC's domestic energy charge starts at ₹3 per unit and rises to ₹6.5 per unit in the highest slab (LT Domestic), plus a monthly fixed charge (latest available estimate). The full slab table is above.
How do I pay my NDMC electricity bill online?
Pay on the official NDMC portal at www.ndmc.gov.in. Use this page first to check what your bill should be, then pay on the authentic source.
