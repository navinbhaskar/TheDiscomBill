Frequently asked questions

How is the electricity bill calculated in Andhra Pradesh?
Andhra Pradesh bills combine slab-wise energy charges, a fixed/demand charge per kW (or kVA), a fuel & power purchase adjustment (FPPA) and electricity duty. Use our free calculator to get an itemised provisional bill for your DISCOM.
Which electricity distribution company serves my area in Andhra Pradesh?
Andhra Pradesh is served by 2 DISCOMs: APSPDCL (Southern AP — Kurnool, Nellore, Prakasam); APEPDCL (Eastern AP — Vishakhapatnam, East Godavari, West Godavari). Open your DISCOM above for its full tariff and an indicative bill.
What is the cheapest domestic electricity rate in Andhra Pradesh?
The lowest domestic energy charge across Andhra Pradesh DISCOMs starts at about ₹1.45 per unit (lowest slab), before fixed charges, FPPA and duty. Exact rates depend on your DISCOM and consumption slab.
