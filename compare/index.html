Calculating tariffs…

What each figure includes

Figures are the estimated total monthly bill — energy + fixed/demand charges + FPPA (where applicable) — for a single-phase connection on the standard urban tariff, computed with the same engine as the calculator and verified against real bills (see our methodology). A fixed 1 kW sanctioned load is assumed across all consumption tiers.

Subsidies & exclusions

Eligible domestic government subsidy is applied where modelled — Delhi (GNCTD), Punjab, Karnataka, Telangana and Tamil Nadu — as free energy on the eligible units. States without a listed scheme aren't subsidised here and may bill lower. Net-metering and late-payment surcharge are excluded.

Reading the table
