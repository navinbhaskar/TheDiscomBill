Bill Review by Experts

Think your electricity bill is wrong — inflated units, a bad meter reading, an unexplained charge? Upload your bill and documents, tell us what's bothering you, and one of our experts will pick it up, examine everything and chat with you right here.

Who reviews your bill?

Independent tariff analysts — the same team that builds and verifies this site's calculation engine against official tariff orders for 65+ DISCOMs. They work with slab rates, FPPA riders, ToD schedules and electricity-duty rules every day.

TheDiscomBill is not affiliated with any DISCOM, so the review answers to you, not the utility. See how we verify our numbers.

How long does it take?

  • Within 24 hours — an expert accepts your case and posts first questions or findings in chat.
  • 2–3 working days — the full audit is typically complete.
  • Complex cases (revised bills, net metering, multiple meters) can take longer — the expert keeps you posted in chat.

The review is free. You only need an account so your documents and chat stay private.

What you get back

  • A line-by-line recheck of energy, fixed, FPPA and duty charges against the current tariff order.
  • Every discrepancy explained in plain language, with the ₹ amount.
  • A clear verdict — overcharged, correct, or undercharged.
  • Concrete next steps if something is wrong: what to tell your DISCOM and which forum to use.
